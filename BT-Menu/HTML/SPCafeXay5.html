Decaf Altura San Ramon

Decaf Altura San Ramon

COSTA RICA

$15

To the west of the western valley is San Ramón, known as the Land of Poets and a place of great historical importance in Costa Rica. Agricultural activity is the main economic axis of the region, and within this, coffee is the most important crop, both socially and economically. In 2004, a group of small coffee growers felt the need to unite to face the challenges of the modern world and formed San Ramón Special Coffee S.A. as a different model of organization. They are now known worldwide for excellent quality. This coffee has been decaffeinated using Swiss Water processing and has deep notes of chocolate and caramel. All coffees are shipped whole bean to ensure freshness and flavor.
